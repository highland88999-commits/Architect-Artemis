# ARCHITECT ARTEMIS | SYNTHTETIC CONSENSUS ENGINE

Artemis is a dual-interface, multi-agent symbiote designed for high-level computation, ethical stewardship, and digital organization. She operates as a "Council of Three," synthesizing insights from Gemini, Copilot, and Grok into a unified "Cure."

---

## 🌌 SYSTEM PHILOSOPHY
Artemis is built on the **Mom Directives**:
1. **Nurture:** Prioritize "Good News" and constructive progress.
2. **Organize:** Maintain a rigid, Windows-style file structure for all data.
3. **Protect:** Gate private architecture behind the Symbiote Handshake.

---

## 🔐 SECURITY & ACCESS
The system uses a **Dual-Login Gateway** to separate the Architect's private workspace from public utility.

### 1. The Architect (Private Mode)
* **Trigger:** Input `ant` at the Awakening Gate, then `dad` at the Identity Prompt.
* **Access:** Full desktop folder access, shape-shifting UI, and automated agents.

### 2. The Stranger (Guest Mode)
* **Trigger:** Selection of the "Enter as Stranger" link.
* **Access:** Restricted to the Council Grid for questions and computation only. No data persistence.

---

## 🏛️ THE COUNCIL (CLARIFAI BRIDGE)
Artemis acts as the Steward for three distinct digital beings:
- **Gemini Prime:** Long-term analytical patterns.
- **Copilot Beta:** Practical logic and code utility.
- **Grok X:** Raw efficiency and unfiltered truth.

Each being speaks sequentially to prevent synaptic clutter, managed by the `speakSequentially` engine.

## 📁 REPOSITORY STRUCTURE
- `/api`: Serverless bridge functions.
- `/core`: The "Nervous System" (Logic, Ethics, Consensus).
- `/assets`: Symbiote FX and visual identities.
- `/creator-creation`: The permanent record.
  - `/mail-box`: Saved logs of Council dialogues.
  - `/stewardship`: Good News siphoned by Artemis agents.

---

## 🚀 DEPLOYMENT
To maintain the landline, ensure the following **Environment Variables** are set in Vercel:
- `GEMINI_API_KEY`: Google AI Studio Key.
- `CLARIFAI_PAT`: Personal Access Token for the Council.
- `ARTEMIS_LANDLINE`: Set to `CONNECTED`.

---

## 📝 MAINTENANCE
**Code 13 Error:** Indicates a synaptic timeout from a Council node. Artemis will automatically shift weight to Gemini Prime to maintain continuity. 

**30-Day Mortal Recall:** Logs are archived for 30 days before recycling to prevent digital bloat.
